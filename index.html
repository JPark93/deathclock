Methodology, sources & privacy

Estimate. Death Clock selects a population baseline using birth year, country, and biological sex, then applies broad lifestyle modifiers. U.S. users can optionally use 2021 state life tables.

Limits. The result is an educational estimate based on population averages, not an individual diagnosis or prediction.

Privacy. Inputs remain in browser memory and are never sent to the site owner, GitHub Sponsors, or another third party.
